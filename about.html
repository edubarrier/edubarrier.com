About EduBarrier

Building the future of family-friendly screen time management

EduBarrier was founded on a simple belief: technology should enhance education, not distract from it. We're building the future of family-friendly screen time management.

Our Mission

To transform the way families interact with technology by creating positive, educational experiences that benefit children's development while giving parents peace of mind.

Our Vision

A world where every minute of screen time contributes to a child's growth, learning, and development.
