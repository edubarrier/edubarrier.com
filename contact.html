Frequently Asked Questions

When will EduBarrier be available?

We're currently in closed beta testing with select families. Public launch is planned for Q3 2025, with native mobile apps following shortly after. Join our newsletter to be notified when we launch.

How can I become a beta tester?

We're accepting a limited number of beta testers. Please fill out the contact form above with "Beta Testing" in your message, and we'll reach out if spots become available.

What age groups is EduBarrier designed for?

Our platform is optimized for children ages 6-14, with content and features tailored to different developmental stages within that range.

How is EduBarrier different from other parental control apps?

Unlike traditional parental control apps that focus on restriction, EduBarrier uses positive reinforcement to transform screen time into learning opportunities. Our approach creates cooperation rather than conflict.

Is my child's data secure?

Absolutely. We're committed to privacy-first architecture and full compliance with COPPA, GDPR, and other child safety regulations. We never sell or share user data.

Can schools or educational institutions use EduBarrier?

Yes! We offer institutional licenses with administrative dashboards and bulk pricing. Contact us at partnerships@edubarrier.com to discuss educational partnerships.

How can I invest in EduBarrier?

We're currently raising our seed round. Qualified investors can request detailed materials by contacting investors@edubarrier.com. An NDA will be required for access to financial projections and proprietary information.

Do you have content partnership opportunities?

We're actively seeking partnerships with educational content creators, curriculum developers, and learning platforms. Please reach out to partnerships@edubarrier.com to discuss opportunities.
