Common Questions from Parents

When will EduBarrier be available?

We're currently testing with select families and planning our public launch for Q3 2025. Join our newsletter to be notified the moment we're ready for new families.

How much does it cost?

We'll announce pricing closer to launch, but we're committed to making EduBarrier accessible to all families. We'll offer flexible plans starting at under $10/month for a single child.

Will my child actually enjoy the educational content?

That's our #1 priority. We've designed content that feels like games and entertainment, not boring homework. Kids earn their recreational screen time by completing lessons they actually want to do.

What ages is EduBarrier designed for?

Our platform works best for children ages 6-14, with content tailored to different grade levels and learning abilities. We're constantly expanding our age range.

Will this work with the apps my child already uses?

EduBarrier works alongside your child's existing apps. They complete educational activities to earn time for the games and apps they love. It's a complement to, not a replacement for, their current experience.

Is my child's information safe?

Absolutely. Privacy and security are non-negotiable for us. We're fully compliant with COPPA and other child safety regulations. We never sell or share your family's data”â€period.

Can I try it before committing?

Yes! We'll offer a free trial period so you can see the difference EduBarrier makes in your home before subscribing. No credit card required.

What if my child tries to work around the system?

We've thought about that. EduBarrier has built-in safeguards to ensure children are actually learning, not just clicking through. Plus, our positive approach means they're motivated to engage genuinely, not game the system.
